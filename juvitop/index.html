We are an informal pedagogical seminar concerning algebraic topology and homotopy theory, localized at Harvard and MIT. For Fall 2025, talks will proceed on Thursdays at 1:30 - 2:30 pm ET, located in SC 309a.

This seminar is organized by Thomas Brazelton and Logan Hyslop, who you should email with questions.

References:

  • Our main reference is the book The norm residue theorem in motivic cohomology by Haesemeyer and Weibel [HW19].
