About Me

I am an NSF Postdoc at Harvard, supervised by Mike Hopkins. I did my PhD at the University of Pennsylvania, and was co-advised by Mona Merling and Kirsten Wickelgren.

I am interested in enumerative geometry and homotopy theory. My CV is here.
