Real curves, $M$-curves, and Hilbert's 16th

I'm collecting links to papers around Hilbert's 16th problem.

  • On the real features of algebraic curves (Original title: Über die reellen Züge algebraischer Kurven), David Hilbert, 1891. [pdf]
  • Die Maximalzahl und Anordnung der Ovale bei der ebenen Kurve 6. Ordnung und bei der Fläche 4. Ordnung., K. Rohn, 1911. [pdf]
    Rohn proves the statement floated by Hilbert in 1891 - that an $M$-curve of degree 6 cannot have all its ovals lying outside each other. Rohn futher proves that no oval contains ten ovals in its interior.
  • On the Topology of Real Plane Algebraic Curves, I. Petrowsky, 1938. [pdf]
    Petrowsky extends the work developed by Rohn in 1911, proving that if $n$ is even, then a curve of order $n$ has at most $\frac{1}{8}(3n^2 - 6n)+1$ ovals exterior to one another. Petrowsky further shows that curves attaining these upper bounds do in fact exist, by Harnack construction. Petrowsky further proves a bound $|P-N| \le \frac{3n^2-6n}{8}+1$ relating the number $P$ of positive ovals and $N$ of negative ovals.
  • On the Arrangement of Ovals of Real Plane Algebraic Curves, Involutions of Four-Dimensional Smooth Manifolds, and the Arithmetic of Integral Quadratic Forms, V.I. Arnol'd, 1971. [translation]
    In this paper, Arnol'd proves Gudkov's conjecture modulo four. The full modulo eight conjecture was later proven by Rokhlin.
  • Proof of Gudkov's hypothesis, V. A. Rokhlin, 1971. [translation]

Survey papers and collections

  • Nine Papers on Hilbert’s 16th Problem, D.A. Gutkov and G.A. Utkin, 1978.
    For the American Mathematical Society Translations Series 2, Gutkov and Utkin collected some of their papers and translated them into English. The included papers are:
    • D. A. Gudkov — Certain theorems on curves of order $m$ [pdf]
    • D. A. Gudkov — Ovals of sixth order curves [pdf]
    • D. A. Gudkov — Systems of $k$ points in general position and algebraic curves of different orders
    • D. A. Gudkov — Properties of rough spaces of sixth order curves with $k$ singular points [pdf]
    • D. A. Gudkov — The change in the topology of a sixth order curve under a continuous change of its coefficients
    • D. A. Gudkov — Complete topological classification of the disposition of ovals of a sixth order curve in the projective plane
    • G. A. Utkin — A connection between fourth order surfaces and sixth order curves
    • G. A. Utkin — The topological classification of nonsingular fourth order surfaces
    • G. A. Utkin — Construction of certain types of nonsingular fourth order surfaces
  • Real algebraic geometry (book), V.I. Arnol'd, 2013.
    Written for Arnold has addressed the book to an open-minded reader who is ready to travel with him in a labyrinth of solved and open problems whose for- mulations are accessible to everyone with a basic knowledge of mathemat- ics, but whose solutions require a certain ingenuity.
